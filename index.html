Our Purpose

Two Good Co. is a social enterprise that provides employment and training opportunities for women escaping domestic violence.

Through our organic food production, catering services, and beautiful design work, we're creating pathways to independence and rebuilding lives. Every purchase supports our mission to create lasting change.
